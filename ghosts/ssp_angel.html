SSP Angel

SSP Angel is a pair of office workers that specialize in providing assistance in the use and development of ukagaka, made by the Angel Team, which includes myself, Ayakamtka, Yuyudev, and Zichqec. It uses the Balloons Balloon balloon.

Features

  • Floaty idle animations
  • Self-inflating
  • Poppable
  • Full of character(s)
  • Helpful functions!
