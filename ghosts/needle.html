Needle

Needle is a living needle made for Ghost Jam 2022 by myself and Claw Grasshammer. It uses the Needloon balloon.

Features

  • No menus
  • No control
  • No logic
  • No options One option* Two options**
  • Endless nicknames
  • Pokes other ghosts

* Due to safety reasons, some dialogues that involve moving text or images are disabled by default. They can be enabled via the enable_extra_content.txt within Needle's files. Please toggle with caution.

** The number of Needle shells available has become so massive that it now has a randomizer for automatically changing shells. This option can be turned on and off if multiple shells are available. Additionally, shells may be included or excluded*** from the randomization list.

*** No, I will not tell you how to do this. Thanks for using Needle!
