Slime

Slime is a slime monster made for Ghost Jam 2018 by myself and Changeside. It uses the blopblop balloon.

Features

  • Soothing idle animations
  • Pettable noggin
  • Feedable gob
  • Provides slime-centric advice
  • Eats straight from your Trash Bin
  • Includes props to decorate your slime's space
