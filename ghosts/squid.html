Squid

Squid is a squid-like creature made for Ghost Jam 2021 by myself and Changeside. It uses the squidloon balloon.

Features

• Energetic idle animations

• Customizable with 30 colors and 5 patterns

• Customizable balloon color when using squidloon

• Unpleasant and demanding

• Soulless stare

• Intrusive pop-ups

• Say a pleasant goodbye, or heartlessly kick squid to the curb when you quit!
