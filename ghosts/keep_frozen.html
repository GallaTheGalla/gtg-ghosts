KEEP FROZEN

Permissions (regarding fanworks, redistribution, etc.)

KEEP FROZEN is a sentient cube of ice made as part of ゴーストマスカレード5 (Ghost Masquerade 5) by myself and Zichqec. It uses the Thaw balloon.

Features

  • 45 minutes of melting per cube to keep you entertained. Economical!

  • Dozens of items to collect and display. Addicting!

  • A face you can toggle on and off, depending on how much guilt you want to feel. Maniacal!

  • Auto-resizing balloon for the most comfortable reading experience. Technological!
